Gramos Dragonlake

June 4, 2023

As I neared the summit, a sight of unparalleled beauty unfolded before my eyes. Nestled in a small valley, an alpine lake glistened like a polished gem, its surface reflecting the world around it with pristine clarity. The waters mirrored the surrounding peaks, creating a mesmerizing illusion of a twin mountain rising from its depths.
