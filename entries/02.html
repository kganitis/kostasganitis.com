Autumn in Steni Forest

Dec 4, 2022

I stepped into the autumn wonderland of Steni forest, where nature's palette had transformed the landscape into a vibrant tapestry. The trees stood tall, adorned with fiery orange and crimson leaves, a stunning contrast against the clear blue sky. As I looked around, I noticed that the ground beneath my feet was carpeted with a mosaic of fallen leaves, their rich hues creating a symphony of colors at my feet.
