Gramos Dragonlake

June 4, 2023

As I neared the summit, a sight of unparalleled beauty unfolded before my eyes. Nestled in a small valley, an alpine lake glistened like a polished gem, its surface reflecting the world around it with pristine clarity. The waters mirrored the surrounding peaks, creating a mesmerizing illusion of a twin mountain rising from its depths.

Autumn in Steni Forest

Dec 4, 2022

I stepped into the autumn wonderland of Steni forest, where nature's palette had transformed the landscape into a vibrant tapestry. The trees stood tall, adorned with fiery orange and crimson leaves, a stunning contrast against the clear blue sky. As I looked around, I noticed that the ground beneath my feet was carpeted with a mosaic of fallen leaves, their rich hues creating a symphony of colors at my feet.

Sturgeon Full Moon in Brauron

Aug 11, 2022

I find myself standing at a picturesque location, nestled by the sea in the suburban area of Brauron in Attica, Greece. The air is warm, carrying the scent of an August summer evening, and the atmosphere is filled with anticipation. I notice some couples scattered around, eagerly awaiting the rise of the full moon. The sound of waves crashing against the shore creates a soothing backdrop as I take in the scene.
